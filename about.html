ABOUT EDUCATOR

In India a lot of children fail to get education due to lack of resources/money. At the same a lot of people passionate about social work lack the opportunity to do so. We at EDUCATOR believe in bringing such people together by providing them with an opportunity to teach the underpriviledged children by partnering with various NGOs.

Educator works with the motive of enabling a brighter future for children from various NGOs by providing them with desired knowledge, skills, values and support to achieve their true potential. We believe that whether you are addressing healthcare, poverty, population control, unemployment or human rights, there's no better place to start than in the corridors of education.

our mission

To educate the children of today to make a better tomorrow.
